About the Project

This web application is part of a group project for the Data Structures and Algorithms course at the Hertie School in Berlin.

Research Question

Are arrests effectively dissuasive against future crime incidents, both over time and across space?

Policy Framing

Shedding light on failures in the justice system and supporting decision-making around police resource allocation.

Target Audience

Policy makers and community leaders.
