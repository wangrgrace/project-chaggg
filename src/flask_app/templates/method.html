cleaning decisions

filter years The year 2001 has a lot of missing values, we therefore decided to filter our data set to range from 2002 - 2025

invalid values When trying to standardise our data used for the algorithm we found the largest distance between two coordinate points to be ~ 600km. As we are trying to predict arrest probabilities within the city of Chicago, we decided to bound our coordinates to the city's limits. We also dropped all instances of missing coordinates.

typos in crime type The raw data included both CRIM SEXUAL ASSAULT and CRIMINAL SEXUAL ASSAULT as primary crime types. After having a closer look at the Illinois Uniform Crime Reporting (IUCR) Codes, we found no record of CRIM SEXUAL ASSAULT being an additonal crime type and therefore renamed it to match the correct spelling.
